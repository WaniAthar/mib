With "Make in India", "Start Up India", "Stand Up India" as the driving power, the journey of MiB in reconstructing the future of the country is accelerated. This unique body strives for the revolutionary evolution of entrepreneurial thinking through the envisioned entrepreneurial ecosystem. Every action of it aims at the profound development of persona of the society. To be precise "Inspiring minds to innovate".

About us

Make in BVB(MIB): With the motto of “Inspiring minds to innovate”, Make in BVB was started by a bunch of self-motivated students under CTIE(Centre for Technology Innovation and Entrepreneurship) in the year 2014. From then till present times it has been instilling entrepreneurial mindset among the youth in and around the state and is recognised even globally through BVB-Umass Student Exchange Programs.

Our Goal

The goal of the club is to inspire students to think innovatively and drive personal and societal development through entrepreneurship. The club provides support and resources for students who are looking to start their own businesses, with the ultimate goal of fostering a culture of innovation and progress on campus.

Entrepreneurial Mindset

To cultivate an entrepreneurial mindset in our club members, we provide a range of resources and support. For example, we offer workshops and seminars on topics such as business planning, market research, and financial management. We also provide networking opportunities and mentorship programs, which can help our members to connect with successful entrepreneurs and learn from their experiences.

In addition to these more formal programs, we also encourage our members to think outside the box and challenge the status quo. We believe that an entrepreneurial mindset is not just about starting businesses, but also about being open to new ideas and taking calculated risks. We encourage our members to think creatively and to be willing to experiment and learn from their mistakes.

Overall, our goal is to create a community of like-minded individuals who are passionate about entrepreneurship and who are committed to driving positive change in the world. We believe that by fostering an entrepreneurial mindset in our members, we can help to shape the future of the country and promote a more innovative and dynamic society.

Leadership quality

We believe that strong leadership is essential for driving personal and societal development. We strive to cultivate leadership qualities in our members by providing them with a range of resources and support.

One of the ways we build leadership qualities is through our mentorship program, which pairs experienced entrepreneurs with aspiring leaders. Through one-on-one interactions, mentees can learn valuable skills and gain insights from their mentors. We also offer workshops and seminars on topics such as communication, teamwork, and decision-making, which can help our members to develop their leadership skills.

In addition to these more formal programs, we also encourage our members to take on leadership roles within the club. This can include organizing events, managing teams, or representing the club in external forums. By taking on these responsibilities, our members can gain valuable experience and develop their leadership abilities.

Overall, our goal is to help our members become confident and capable leaders who are ready to drive positive change in the world. We believe that by fostering strong leadership qualities in our members, we can contribute to the development of a more dynamic and successful society.

Cultivating Innovation

We strive to foster a culture of innovation in our club by providing our members with the tools and support they need to think creatively and develop novel ideas.

One of the ways we encourage innovation in our club is through our workshops and seminars, which focus on topics such as design thinking, problem-solving, and idea generation. These sessions provide our members with the knowledge and skills they need to approach challenges in a creative and innovative way.

In addition to these more formal programs, we also encourage our members to experiment and take risks. We believe that innovation often comes from trying new things and being willing to fail. To support this approach, we provide resources such as prototyping materials and access to makerspaces, which can help our members to bring their ideas to life.

Overall, our goal is to create a community of innovators who are passionate about driving positive change in the world. By fostering a culture of innovation in our club, we hope to inspire our members to think creatively and develop solutions to the challenges facing our society.

What is PUPA?

Ever had a superb idea but didn’t know how to go about it? This is your chance!! PUPA is a soaring entrepreneurial experience blended with technical knowledge that the students bring to the table. This incredible event is organized and executed by E-KLETech students. As the name suggests, PUPA is a transient yet thriving state where student teams accumulate essential resources and exhibit their final products within four weeks.

Saga

Being held for the last 9 years, PUPA has gathered attention from institutions all over Karnataka. A journey that began with 20 budding ideas in 2013, has now reached over 500 engineered ideas from students of 32 institutions across North Karnataka including 4 schools, 3 polytechnic colleges, 5 MBA institutions, 2 PU colleges, and 16 engineering institutions. Each team was given a seed capital and the teams came up with their best ideas and exhibited them at KLE Tech University. PUPA is a versatile event for innovative ideas with the prime aspect to emphasize the entrepreneurial facet of students.
